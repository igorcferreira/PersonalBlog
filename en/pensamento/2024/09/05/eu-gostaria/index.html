Writing from the past - I would like

05 Sep 2024

23/04/2011 - I would like…

“I would like to be able to apologize, say thank you, and proclaim to the seven winds what my heart has carried for so long. Could I gather enough courage to put my soul at risk and look into your beautiful eyes while I free myself from this weight that consumes me? I’m sorry for saying what I said and doing what I did, but turning myself into a monster to your senses was the best way to push you away from my eyes, since I will never be able to push you away from my thoughts.”

Igor Castañeda Ferreira
