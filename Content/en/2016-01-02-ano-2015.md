For years, my New Year's resolutions have amounted to:

`
Goals for year n:
Prepare goals for year n+1
`

I never planned anything more than a month ahead. To be honest, I never planned anything in my life.

I believe the main factors for this (among many) are: my lack of ambition, detachment from many things, and the security I have in family and friends.

I've always had the freedom to take risks and do what I thought I wanted to do (something I didn't take advantage of as I should have), knowing that if something went wrong, I would have friendly shoulders to turn to. This allowed me to try various things, like music, theatre, electronics, game development, etc. As well as various trips and job changes. ^^

*This, however, did not exclude my "shyness" and the profile categorized by many as introverted, which accompanies me.*



## What changed in 2015
---

As the ad would say, everything changed. 2015 was a year where my life took a big turn.

The main one happened like everything else in my life, with the support of family and friends: I moved to London, where I got a job at [Future Workshops](http://futureworkshops.com/). I admit that the decision to take this step was not easy (in fact, I had been postponing this step for a long time).

What made me look at, consider, and accept this change was the year 2015. This year, more trips, where I talked with 3 companies outside Brazil about the possibility of leaving the country. This, added to the economic situation of the country, made me decide.

I received criticism for this decision from a few people and most were happy that I took this step, always reminding me that homesickness would be strong, and indeed, it weighs heavily. DAILY (but that will be a topic for another day)!

2. I would have to take 7 days off before the end of the year.
3. I decided to visit the county where my grandfather was born.
